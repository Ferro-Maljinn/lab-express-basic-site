About Me

Hi, I'm Peter Draws, and I enjoy drawing. My real name is Peter Deligdisch. Here is where I do primarily timelapse drawing videos. I draw, and then I speed up the footage using Adobe Premiere Pro and record a little commentary to put over it. My favorite pens are probably Rotring Isographs, and I like Crescent Rendr and Moleskine Sketchbooks, or Bristol paper for loose-leaf. I also enjoy a wide variety of fountain pens, glass dip pens, etc. I just like drawing lines! I also attempt other types of art from time to time as well, just to keep things fresh. Let me know if you have any questions, thanks for stopping by!
